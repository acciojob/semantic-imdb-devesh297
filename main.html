Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. He is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully plant an idea in someone's mind.

Main Actors

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
